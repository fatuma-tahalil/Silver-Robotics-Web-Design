    Our Team

    Founded in 2024, we are a Canadian FRC team based in Silverthorn Collegiate Institute. Competing in the Ontario District, we have attended McMasters and Humber. Our goal is to teach students technical and non-technical skills that give students the confidence to succeed. All while having fun in a freindly competition.
