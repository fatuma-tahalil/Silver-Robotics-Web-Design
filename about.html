    • ABOUT

    Silverthorn Collegiate Institute is a publicly-funded secondary school located in the Etobicoke region of Toronto, Ontario, Canada, which has special programs for junior elite athletes and for students interested in pursuing careers in the arts. FRC Team 9575 is the first STEM-oriented club or team at SCI. The population of the school is approximately 850 students who embrace the school values to become the Spartans they are expected to be, by displaying self-discipline, positive attitude, acceptance, resilience, trustworthiness, authenticity, nobility, and school spirit.

    MISSION

    Team 9575 has the mission to create a friendly and motivating environment for people who like working with robotics in general. We want to give students of all grades the opportunity to gain experience in collaboration, leadership, communication, and so on. Our team consists of people from all kinds of backgrounds, so it would be an excellent chance for students to develop social networking skills and connections. Our goal in competitions would provide wonderful experience dealing with challenges, overcoming issues, as well as taking responsibilities. Team 9575 promises all members helpful and interesting moments with engineering, mechanics, and management.
